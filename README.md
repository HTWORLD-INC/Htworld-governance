# HTWORLD — Governance, Legal, and Policy Repository

This repository is the authoritative source for HTWORLD Corporation’s governance, legal, and policy documents. It is maintained to provide transparency, consistency, and a single point of reference for HTWORLD Corporation and its controlled subsidiaries, affiliates, and associated entities (collectively, the “HTWORLD Group”).

Documents in this repository may be incorporated by reference into contracts, statements of work, service orders, invoices, and other instruments issued by HTWORLD Group entities, as applicable.

---

## Purpose and Structure

HTWORLD Corporation serves as the parent, steward, and coordinating entity of the HTWORLD Group. This repository publishes group‑level policies and framework terms that may be:

- Adopted by HTWORLD Corporation directly, or
- Incorporated by reference by HTWORLD Group entities that provide operational services or conduct regulated activities.

Each HTWORLD Group entity remains a separate legal entity and is independently responsible for its own operations, contracting, and compliance obligations, except as expressly stated in a written agreement.

## Core Documents

### Master Service Framework Terms
**File:** `terms.md`  
**Description:**  
Group‑level master service framework terms establishing baseline contractual provisions for services provided by HTWORLD Group entities. The applicable contracting entity is identified in the relevant order, statement of work, or invoice.

When adopted by reference, these terms govern over conflicting customer purchase order terms unless expressly overridden in a signed agreement.

### Privacy Notice
**File:** `privacy.md`  
**Description:**  
HTWORLD Group Privacy Notice describing privacy practices applicable to HTWORLD Corporation’s informational presence and establishing a framework that may be adopted by subsidiaries and affiliates, subject to entity‑specific supplements where required.
